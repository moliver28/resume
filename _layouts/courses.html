Design of Digital Circuits and Systems

Role: Teaching Assistant
Description

An intermediate level course in the design and development of digital /embedded systems. The main objective of the class is to provide students with a theoretical background to and practical experience with the tools, techniques, and methods for solving challenges related to modeling complex systems using the Verilog hardware description language (HDL), signal integrity, managing power consumption in digital systems, and ensuring robust intra and inter system communication working with systems of midlevel complexity
